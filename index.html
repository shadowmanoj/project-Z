WHAT ?

Do you ever felt happy seeing green light when stuck in huge traffic? Yes, in today’s life watching red light turning into green feels contented. Why not this happiness last for a quite while? The same thought led us in modelling a new traffic architecture. Traffic has become an inescapable problem in many major cities. The time people waiting infront of red light is increasing day by day. Why is it getting worse? The current traffic architecture works on pre-defined static time allocation. This approach works good for even traffic but fails with uneven traffic. There are many approaches that speaks about controlling congestion of traffic but very few focused on harnessing time wasted in current architecture. We examined many of the existing methods and deliberate a new traffic management system which is smart, automated and effective.

HOW ?

How do we increase green light time? Time is eternal. We can’t just add green light time to a certain road by making other traffic wait but we can utilize the time actually wasted in current traffic architecture. This can be achieved by taking real instance of traffic into consideration. Our flow of execution starts by using available surveillance cameras adjusted to desired heights to capture frames then processing them to get actual severity of traffic. Many methods were successful till here calculating the time required for clearance of detected traffic. But that’s not enough. So, we dived deep into further optimizations called variance-based optimization which compares the traffic on all other sides of the signal with current road then altering the time allotted previously. This method not only optimizes the first result but also ensure that no time is wasted in the process. The algorithm is finally tuned for protecting mean-time of the vehicles by readjusting the variance times if they exceed the maximum waiting time. By this every vehicle in the first row of signal will be promised to pass the signal within mean time. These 3 phases of optimization increases the real-time effectiveness and helps in minimizing uneven traffic. Excited to learn more? Feel free to access our research paper here

WHAT ELSE?

We Speak Facts. Not Fantasies

Yes, we speak only facts. We established ourselves to project away from traditional approaches by modeling a new type of traffic architecture. Although the results obtained were much better compared to current architectures, still our way of execution should be enhanced further. Shortage of desired datasets and execution speeds limited us to output a working content rather than making it perfect. With enough datasets and acceptable time constraints, our algorithm can be further modified to join real-time success. Our work makes sense when we are successful in saving a minute of wasted time because every second counts.
