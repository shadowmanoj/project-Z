An Effective Solution to uneven traffic using Artificial Intilligence.

WHAT ?

Traffic congestion is one of the most vexing urban issues. Population growth together with a great demand of transportation indirectly contribute to the increasing number of vehicles on the road that will eventually cause traffic congestion. Many traditional and recent approaches tried to decrease traffic congestion by rerouting vehicles, increasing width of roads, developing new architectures etc, but we are trying to touch on different side of approach which focuses on time wasted in Today's traffic.

HOW ?

The execution of algorithm begins with detecting vehicles on the road for which green light will be alloted in near future. Each category is alloted with specific set of points. Based on the total points a road gets, the green light for vehicle clearance will be calculated. Many methods were successful till here but we are further optimising it. The vehicles densities are compared with other roads thereby varying the alloted green light time. This increases real-time effectiveness and helps in minimising uneven traffic. The final method focuses on protecting mean time. Every vehicle in the first row of the signal is promised to cross the signal within mean time no matter whatever optimisation is carried out.
